# Swallowing the Anchor

*For Captain Noor Mohammed, Indian Navy — 1 July 1992 to 31 March 2026*

---

A personal tribute published at midnight on 31 March 2026, the night my father retired from the Indian Navy after thirty-three years and nine months of service.

The title comes from the old sailor's phrase for leaving the sea for the last time — swallowing the anchor.

**Live at:** https://goofbol.github.io/anchor
